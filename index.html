About us

Welcome to Avyagraha Research and Analytics LLP

We are a deep-tech innovation and research company specialising in statistics, data analytics, artificial intelligence, agentic AI, quantum computing, bioinformatics and AI-driven automation. Founded in 2025, we bridge research, technology and real-world impact.

Our mission is to empower businesses, researchers and students with advanced analytical capabilities, while building the next generation of technology talent through training, collaboration and applied research.

Closing the gaps in research and innovation

Three recurring problems keep good data from becoming good decisions. We are built to close them.

Organisations struggle to access skilled talent in AI, quantum computing, bioinformatics and advanced analytics for complex projects.

Academia to industry skill gap

Students often lack hands-on exposure to real-world applications, while industries face shortages of trained, job-ready professionals.

Underused data and inefficiency

Businesses hold valuable data but lack the automation, analytics and consulting support to convert it into strategic insight.
